Player 1 - Arrow keys to move, ? to change weapons, and Shift to shoot.
Player 2 - W,A,S, and D to move, E to change weapons, and Q to shoot

Shoot all of the monsters (green squares) to finish each round. If either player gets hit by a monster, you will lose so avoid them at all costs.

Weapons (red lines) with different abilities will drop around the map. Walk over them to pick them up. They have limited ammunition so be sure to use them wisely. You'll need them to beat the game.
